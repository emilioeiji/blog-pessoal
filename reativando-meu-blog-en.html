Reactivating My Blog

Hello everyone! I'm thrilled to announce the reactivation of my blog after a period of rest. Throughout this time, I've been dedicated to studying technology and acquiring valuable knowledge that I'll be sharing with all of you.

In this new chapter, I'll be bringing relevant content about the world of technology and reflections from my day-to-day experiences. I believe that this combination will enrich our discussions and provide an even more rewarding exchange.

I've decided to adopt the use of a static site generator called Pelican. I chose this technology due to its simplicity, efficiency, and the ability to focus more on the content itself. To ensure an isolated and organized environment, I'm utilizing virtual environments (venv) for the installation of Pelican.

As for hosting, I've chosen to use a Virtual Private Server (VPS) on DigitalOcean. This will give me full control over the hosting environment, in addition to offering scalability and reliability for my blog.

In the upcoming posts, I intend to share the step-by-step process of installing Pelican using venv, as well as details on how I configured hosting on DigitalOcean's VPS. I hope this journey can inspire and assist those who are also interested in starting their own online projects.

I want to express my gratitude to everyone who has followed the blog up to this point, and I extend a warm welcome to new readers who will be joining us.
